1.3 Where did the PFP take place?

Crow Canyon Archaeological Center’s rural campus in Cortez, Colorado.

Five PFP gardens are scattered around Crow Canyon Archaeological Center’s rural campus in Cortez, Colorado; In 2015, we planted a new garden in the “bean fields” north of Crow Canyon’s campus. Use the map below to explore the PFP garden locations, and click on the gardens to get more information about when they were planted as part of PFP research.
